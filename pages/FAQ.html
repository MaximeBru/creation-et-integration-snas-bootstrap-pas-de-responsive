Frequently Asked Questions:

  • Q1: When was Vermeg founded?

    Vermeg was founded in 1994.
    In 2014, the merger of the two companies "Vermeg and the company Business solutions builders international SA "BSB" allowed the birth of a new identity "Vermeg Group"


    Vermeg Group

  • Q2: Which are the various centers of expertise in which Vermeg specialized?

    Professionals working in main centers of expertise:
    • Packaged software solutions:
      • VERMEG Securities for financial securities processing.(Formely Megara)
      • VERMEG Wealth Management and VERMEG Institutional Management for asset & wealth management.(Formely Soliam)
      • VERMEG Life for life insurance(Formely Solife)
    • And IT development framework solution: VERMEG Tecnology enabling bespoke development of reusable componements and IT solutions.


    Vermeg Group

  • Q3: Where did "Vermeg" names come from?

    Being fascinated by art and paint, our Chairman Badreddine Ouali has been inspired by the Dutch baroque painter "Johannes Vermeer" and the carthaginian site "Megara" to create Vermeg's company name.


    Vermeg Group

  • Q4: Where did "Palmyra" names come from?

    Palmyra's name was inspired by the name "Palmyre" which is a Syrian archaeological site that houses the monumental ruins of a great city that was one of the most important cultural centers of the ancient world
    This nod to Syria was inspired by our Vice Chairman Marwan Hanifeh.


    Vermeg Group

  • Q5: Where did "Megara" names come from?

    Megara was a trendy neighbourhood when Carthage dominated south half of the Mediterranean Sea.
    Peaople who lived there were extremely fortunate.


    Vermeg Group
